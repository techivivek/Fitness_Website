Bulking Diet Plan

Follow this structured plan to gain muscle mass in a healthy and effective way.

Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs + Oatmeal with banana & peanut butter
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Protein shake + Almonds
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast + Brown rice + Steamed broccoli
  • Afternoon Snack: Cottage cheese + Whole wheat toast
  • Dinner: Salmon + Quinoa + Avocado salad
  • Evening Snack: Greek yogurt + Nuts

Training Plan

  • Strength Training: 5-6 days per week (Heavy lifting with progressive overload)
  • Compound Movements: Squats, Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-ups
  • Moderate Cardio: 2-3 times per week (Light jogging, Stair climbing)
  • Rest Days: 1-2 days per week for muscle recovery
