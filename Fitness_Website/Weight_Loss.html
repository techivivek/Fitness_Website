Weight Loss Diet Plan

Follow this structured plan to help you lose weight in a healthy and sustainable way.

Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with nuts & berries + Green tea
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt + Almonds
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast + Quinoa + Steamed vegetables
  • Afternoon Snack: Apple + Peanut butter
  • Dinner: Grilled salmon + Spinach salad + Olive oil dressing
  • Evening Snack: Herbal tea + Handful of walnuts

Training Plan

  • Cardio: 30-40 minutes of running or cycling
  • Strength Training: 4 days per week (Full-body workouts)
  • HIIT Workouts: 2 days per week (Jump squats, Burpees, Plank)
  • Rest Days: 1-2 days per week with light yoga or stretching
